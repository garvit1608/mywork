Short Movies

The first product that we launched on 16.12.2013 was an one hour length documentary on the Delhi Rape Incident. So as we started through a video, we tried to incorporate these skills into every other parallel product such as The Debate Speak Up Speak Now and our social ventures. We are also developing short films in multiple genres for entertainment and awareness purposes.Our YouTube channel is the repository of all future episodes.

Photography

The photography domain of the organization promotes budding photographers to submit their work which we use in some of our work and also provide them with their own space to display their own collection. The photography team organizes photo walks which are random and extensive photo shoots in a particular area of locality, naturale, etc. The team is also responsible for covering the organization’s video products.

Web Novel

Everyone has attempted to write a diary at some or the other point in his/her life. This product tries to unite these experiences and create a story which is an aggregation of our own experiences during school, college and so on. The writing hunger of certain creative people is utilized here when we develop a character, the back story and plan to move forward with the story as the writers of a popular tv show. We are planning to create a live product with regular updates from our writers and create characters that the reader can associate with easily. The team is currently developing the characters and the back story and the product will be put on the website very soon. The plan is to give the writers an opportunity to produce and manage a novel and look where the product goes!!!

Online Magazine

A youth oriented magazine is another product down the line. The magazine will not just be a representation of all products and activities of ENSEMBLE but will also focus on news articles and current affairs; all written in a very youth oriented pattern. The concept of the magazine is to develop it as a comic book. Again the writers can submit any literary article. We will try to encourage them to be more active in poetry and literary activities to get them away from the conventional status updates to the old techniques of pen and paper.

Social

The team is also aware of its social obligations being a citizen of this country; a citizen who is more fortunate than many. There are regular visits planned to government schools where the volunteers spend time with the students, advise them on their career choices and motivate them to never consider themselves as not in the race. Also the team is planning to spend festivals with orphans and people in old age homes. The members of the domain are coming up with new ideas which make a direct impact to the society.

Vallalar Gurukulam

We have been fascinated by the work of this orphanage which supports 20-30 school going orphans who have drifted from their real homes and native places. The fact that touched us was that this orphanage was started by a taxi driver who drives during the day and not just supports his own family but also his extended family. They all live under the same roof and he and his wife consider all the orphans as their own children. We were mesmerized to see such compassion from such unexpected of places.
We are currently developing a documentary on this orphanage to generate some funds for them. Also we visit the orphanage at regular intervals to spend time with the family.
